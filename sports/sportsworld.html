Summary

Some states have teams from all five leagues (NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, WNBA), while others only have teams in one or a few leagues.

  • States with teams in all five leagues: Arizona, California, Illinois, Minnesota,Texas, New York and Wasington.

  • States without any major sports teams: Alaska, Hawaii, Montana, South Dakota, West Virginia, and others. More than 50% of the states do not have any sports teams.
